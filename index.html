Hello World!

ZyDev_

Greetings, I am Antoine Landrieux, a connoisseur of the intricate world of programming languages. At the age of 8, my fascination with coding ignited a spark within me, leading me to craft a programming masterpiece known as LDRX. As my journey unfolded, my curiosity expanded to the realms of operating systems, where I now delve into the realm of innovation with my latest creation, the illustrious OS BORIUM. My passion for programming has been a guiding force throughout my life, shaping my perspective and fueling my creativity. The art of coding has become not just a skill but a form of expression, a medium through which I can bring my ideas to life. From the humble beginnings of learning basic syntax to the intricate complexities of software architecture, each line of code I write tells a story of dedication and determination. As I navigated the complexities of kernel development, device drivers, and system calls, I found myself immersed in a world of bytes and bits, where every line of code had the power to shape the behavior of the entire system. The challenges were daunting, but the rewards were immense, as each hurdle overcome brought me one step closer to realizing my vision of a lean, efficient, and reliable operating system.
